Consortium Indicator
Review Tool

Multi-partner consortia routinely submit indicators that overlap — but don't match. Different measurement periods, divergent definitions, misaligned baselines. This tool compares each partner's indicator submissions field-by-field against a designated Reference agency, surfaces reporting risks, drafts harmonized positions, and produces a documented decision trail for audits and donor reporting. It runs entirely in Google Sheets, with an optional AI layer that drafts the analytical content for your review.

How It Works

The tool runs as a four-step workflow inside Google Sheets. Each step reads from the previous one — run them in order.

1

Compare Indicators

Reads all intake data and diffs every field against the Reference agency. Skips any indicator with no Reference agency row and flags it with a toast notification. Always runs via script — no manual option for this step.

2

Check Reporting Risk

Produces one row per flagged difference. In AI mode, drafts the Risk Type, Description, and Conditions. You set the final Risk Level (High / Medium / Low / None) — your edits carry forward to Step 3.

3

Draft Harmonized Position

In AI mode, drafts a field-level harmonized position starting from the Reference agency's exact text, deviating only where a partner difference reveals a technical problem. Filter by High/Medium risk to prioritize your review.

4

Finalize Review

In AI mode, drafts decision framing fields. You complete the Residual Risk Decision, Governance Notes, and Final Status for each field: Approved / Approved with Conditions / Revise / Do Not Standardize.
